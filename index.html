About Me

Hi, I am Zilu Meng, a Ph.D. student working with Prof. Gregory J. Hakim and Prof. Eric J. Steig at the University of Washington. Before that, I received my B.S. in Atmospheric Sciences from Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology and did research on tropical dynamics with Prof. Tim Li. I am interested in Paleoclimate, Machine learning, Climate dynamics, and Data assimilation. I am currently working on Paleoclimate Data Assimilation, Last Millennium Climate Dynamics. In my free time, I enjoy vedio games, reading, and hiking.
